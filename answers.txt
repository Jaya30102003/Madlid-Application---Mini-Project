In a distant Jungle, there lived an adventurous Explorer known as Sara. Accompanied by a faithful Guide, a majestic Tiger named Roxy, Sara roamed freely across the Savannah. One fateful day, Sara decided to undertake a perilous Expedition. Equipped with only a sturdy Map, Sara ventured into the uncharted Wilderness. Along the way, an enigmatic Shaman appeared, offering an ancient Amulet in exchange for a rare Map. Will Sara accept the offer and press on with the Quest, or will doubt linger in Sara's heart, leading to a retreat back Campsite?
